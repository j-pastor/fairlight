### What are *Fairlight ‚Äì A Prelude* and *Fairlight II ‚Äì A Trail of Darkness*?

*Fairlight ‚Äì A Prelude* and *Fairlight II ‚Äì A Trail of Darkness* are classic isometric adventure games originally developed in the 1980s for 8-bit home computers, including the ZX Spectrum, Amstrad CPC, Amstrad PCW, and Commodore 64.

They are widely regarded as landmark titles due to their advanced engine design, emergent gameplay mechanics, and technical ambition on very limited hardware.

---

### Who created the original games?

Both games were created and programmed by **Bo Jangeborg**, who was responsible for the game design, programming, and the underlying engine technology.

---

### Is this an official remake or remaster?

No.  
This project is a **PC port**, not a remake or remaster. Its goal is to preserve and make the original games playable on modern systems, while remaining faithful to their original design and mechanics.

### Which engine is used for this PC port?

The project has been developed using **Godot Engine 4.5**.

---

### On which platforms does the PC port run?

Precompiled executables are provided for:

- Windows  
- Linux  
- macOS  

The project can also be built manually using Godot.

---

### Can I contribute improvements or fixes?

Yes.  
Contributions such as bug fixes, refactoring, performance improvements, or tooling enhancements are welcome, provided they respect the license terms and do not introduce unauthorized Fairlight content.

---

### Is this project intended as a learning resource?

Absolutely.

One of the goals of this project is to serve as a **learning and experimentation resource** for developers interested in:

- Isometric engines  
- Game preservation  
- Reimplementing classic game mechanics  
- Godot Engine development
